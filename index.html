Good To Know

Questions we get every spring.

When's the best time to verti-mow in Perth? {{ faqIcon0 }}

Spring. September to November is the sweet spot: the soil is warming up, your lawn is actively growing, and it has the whole season to recover and thicken up before the summer heat arrives.

Will my lawn look rough straight after? {{ faqIcon1 }}

For a week or two, yes. Dethatching is aggressive by design, and that short, scalped look is completely normal. With water and a feed behind it, most lawns come back noticeably thicker and greener within a few weeks.

What's in the Complete Rejuvenation Package? {{ faqIcon2 }}

Dethatching, aeration, topdressing, fertilising, wetting agent and a reticulation check: the full reset, done in one booking.

How do quotes work? {{ faqIcon3 }}

They're free, and you don't need to be home. Send your details and a few photos online or via Facebook and we'll price it up. Prefer an on-site assessment? We do those too. EFTPOS available on the day.

Is vertimowing safe for buffalo grass? {{ faqIcon4 }}

Yes, but the depth matters. Buffalo (Sir Walter, Palmetto, Sapphire) is a surface-runner and doesn't tolerate being cut right to the soil, so we set the blades shallower than we do for couch, removing the thatch without scalping the runners. Couch handles a much deeper cut and actually responds better to it. Not sure what grass you have? Send a photo with your quote and we'll tell you.
