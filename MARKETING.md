## PART 1 — How to get to the top of Google

For a local service like yours, Google actually has **two** "tops":

1. **The Map Pack** (the 3 businesses shown with the map, above the normal results).
   This is where the phone calls come from. It is won mostly by your **Google
   Business Profile + reviews**, NOT your website.
2. **The blue links** below it. This is won by your website (the SEO tags, content
   and speed — most of which are already done for you in `index.html`).

Do them in this order.

### 1. Set up your Google Business Profile (biggest win, do it today) ⭐

This is free and it is the single most important thing for a Perth lawn business.

1. Go to **google.com/business** and sign in with a Google account.
2. Business name: **Perth Lawn Renovation Services** (exactly as on the website).
3. Category: primary = **Lawn care service**. Add secondary categories:
   *Landscaper*, *Gardener*.
4. You're a service-area business (you go to customers), so **hide your address**
   and set the **service area** to your suburbs: Joondalup, Wanneroo, Stirling,
   Fremantle, Canning, Cockburn, Rockingham, Mandurah, Kalamunda, Armadale, etc.
5. Phone: **0427 206 246**. Website: **https://perthlawnrenovationservices.com.au**.
6. Hours: Mon–Sat 7:00–17:00.
7. **Verify** the profile (postcard, phone or video). You cannot rank until verified.
8. Upload **at least 10 photos**: your best before/afters, the gear, action shots.
   Google favours profiles with lots of real photos. Use the ones in `/assets`.
9. Post a weekly "Update" (a photo of a recent job + one line). Takes 2 minutes,
   keeps the profile "active", which Google rewards.

### 2. Get reviews — this is the ranking fuel ⭐

The Map Pack order is driven heavily by **number of reviews + recency + rating**.
A competitor with 40 reviews will beat a better business with 3. So:

- After **every** job, text the customer your Google review link. Script:
  > "Thanks again for having us out! If you're happy with the lawn, a quick Google
  > review would genuinely help a small local business. Takes 30 seconds: [link]"
- Get the short review link from your Business Profile → **Ask for reviews** →
  copy link. Save it as a text shortcut on your phone.
- Aim for **1–2 new reviews a week**. That pace alone will move you up the Map Pack.
- **Always reply** to every review (even a thumbs up). Replies are a ranking signal
  and take 20 seconds.
- ⚠️ Never buy or fake reviews — Google removes them and can suspend the profile,
  and fake reviews breach Australian Consumer Law.

> Reminder from your README: the three sample reviews on the website (Jason M.,
> Sarah K., Mark T.) are placeholder copy. Swap them for real customer quotes
> before pointing ads at the site.

### 3. Local citations (NAP consistency)

Google trusts you more when your **N**ame, **A**ddress/area, **P**hone appear
identically across the web. List the business — same name, same 0427 206 246 —
on the free directories:

- Yellow Pages AU, True Local, Hotfrog, Yelp AU, StartLocal, Aussie Web,
  Word of Mouth (womow.com.au — great for trades), Localsearch.
- Your Facebook page (you already have one — make sure the phone + website match).

Consistency matters more than quantity. Use **Perth Lawn Renovation Services**
and **0427 206 246** every single time.

### 4. Your website is already optimised — here's what's done

In this repo I've set up the technical SEO so you don't have to touch code:

- ✅ Title, meta description, keywords tuned for "vertimowing Perth / lawn
  renovation Perth".
- ✅ **LocalBusiness structured data** with your services, prices, area, hours,
  logo and geo — this is what lets Google show rich info.
- ✅ **FAQ structured data** matching the on-page FAQ.
- ✅ `sitemap.xml` and `robots.txt` so Google can find and crawl every page.
- ✅ Mobile-friendly, fast, one canonical URL.

**One thing you must do manually (10 min, free):**

1. Go to **Google Search Console** (search.google.com/search-console).
2. Add property → enter `perthlawnrenovationservices.com.au`.
3. Verify (easiest: it may auto-verify via your domain registrar, or add the DNS
   TXT record they give you).
4. Once verified: **Sitemaps** → submit `sitemap.xml`.
5. Use **URL Inspection** → paste your homepage → **Request indexing**.

That tells Google your site exists today instead of waiting weeks to be found.

### 5. Content that ranks (the slow-burn wins)

Google ranks pages that answer what people **type**. Perth homeowners search things
like *"when to vertimow couch lawn Perth"*, *"how much does dethatching cost Perth"*,
*"buffalo lawn renovation Perth"*. You already answer these in your FAQ — over time,
consider adding a simple blog/tips page targeting one question each. Even 4–5 short
articles (400–600 words) aimed at real questions will pull in searches your
competitors ignore. Not urgent — do #1–4 first.

### How to actually run the ads (cheap and effective)

1. **Start with "Boost Post", not the full Ads Manager.** On your Facebook page,
   post Ad 1 (with the before/after image), then hit **Boost**.
2. **Audience:** location = Perth + 15km (or pick your suburbs). Age 30–65+.
   Interests: *gardening, home improvement, home ownership*. This keeps you in
   front of people who own a lawn.
3. **Budget:** $5–$10/day is plenty to start. Run it 5–7 days, see which ad gets
   the cheapest messages/clicks, then put more behind the winner.
4. **Send them somewhere that converts:** link to
   `perthlawnrenovationservices.com.au` (the instant quote) OR set the button to
   **"Send Message"** so leads land in Messenger.
5. **Best time to spend:** late winter into spring (Aug–Nov) is your peak. That's
   when Perth homeowners look at their lawn and decide to fix it.
6. **Always use a real before/after photo** as the image — it out-performs
   everything else for lawn work.
